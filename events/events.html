Events

DAY 1 -
NOVEMBER 16

Event Kick-off

This day will be the start of the event. The Clima Festival will be introduced along with the 350 organization. We will be talking about what we stand for and our purpose. There will also be talks and conversations with Clima Festival and 350 members.

DAY 2 -
NOVEMBER 17

The Science

Hear from climate scientists. Climate scientists will be informing us of what Climate Change is and the overall effects of Climate Change on the Earth, our lives, and the future.

Day 3 -
NOVEMBER 18

Let's Talk About It

On this day, celebrities that are Climate Change activists will give talks. Some of the celebrities include Leonardio Dicaprio, Shailene Woodley, Jane Fonda, Meryl Streep, Ben Affleck, and Mark Ruffalo.

Day 4 -
NOVEMBER 19

Game On

Activities will be held in order to teach methods of combating climate change. Contestants will compete in different games and winners will receive prizes.

Day 5 -
NOVEMBER 20

Music to my Ears

To close out the festival, conerts will be held by different musicians that support the cause against the Climate Crisis.
