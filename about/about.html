What is the Clima Festival?

The Clima Festival is a yearly festival that is hosted at the Stern Grove in San Francisco, California. The Festival started in 2020 and is an event whose main purpose is to raise awareness towards climate change. The Clima Festival partners with climate consicous organizations like 350 in order to help the organzitation gain more funding and attention.


What is the 350 Organziation?

350 is an international organization that focuses on stopping the use of fossil fuels in order to build a more sustainable future. The 350 organization is active across 70+ countries with a 10,000+ total active members. They value justice for the people that have been impacted the most and holding the ones that have had the biggest impact on climate change to be accountable.
